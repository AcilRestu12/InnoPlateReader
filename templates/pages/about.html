About InnoPlateReader

Welcome to InnoPlateReader! Our mission is to provide an efficient and accurate solution for Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR) using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms.

What is InnoPlateReader?

InnoPlateReader is an advanced system designed to detect and read vehicle license plates from images. Our system leverages the power of the YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once) object detection algorithm and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to ensure high accuracy and fast processing times.

Our Mission

At InnoPlateReader, we aim to make automatic license plate recognition accessible and reliable for everyone. Whether for security, toll collection, parking management, or any other application, our goal is to provide a robust solution that meets your needs.

Key Features

Using the YOLOv8 algorithm, InnoPlateReader accurately detects the location of license plates in uploaded images.

After detecting the license plate, our OCR module reads the characters on the plate with high precision.

Our web interface, built with TailwindCSS and Flask, allows users to easily interact with the system.

Users can upload new datasets to improve the model's performance, set training parameters, and view training results, including the confusion matrix.
